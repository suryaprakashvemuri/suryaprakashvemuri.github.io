About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of North Texas, joining the HEAL Lab under the supervision of Dr. Mahdi Pedram. My research focuses on Health-embedded AI, bridging the gap between embedded sensing and ML-driven analytics to create intelligent, sustainable infrastructure.

Previously, I served as a Graduate Research Assistant at the University of the Pacific, contributing to smart solar and agrivoltaic systems. With a background in IoT-enabled cyber-physical systems, I am passionate about developing robust, real-world deployments that provide actionable insights through on-device intelligence.
